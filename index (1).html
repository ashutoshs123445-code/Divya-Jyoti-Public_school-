Our Story

Legacy & Vision

History & Establishment

Founded in 2010, Divya Jyoti Public School was established with a powerful vision: to bring world-class, holistic education to the students of Chandauli. From our humble beginnings, we have evolved into a premier educational institution, continuously pushing the boundaries of academic and extracurricular excellence.

Principal's Vision

Mr. SHASHWAT PANDEY — Principal, DJPS

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At Divya Jyoti, we don't just teach the syllabus — we build character, instill core values, and prepare our students to be leaders of tomorrow."

Curriculum

Academics

Our curriculum is deeply rooted in CBSE guidelines, integrated with modern, tech-driven teaching methodologies.

  • Pre-Primary (Nursery–UKG): Play-way methodologies, basic cognitive skills, and interactive activities.
  • Primary & Middle (Class 1–8): NCERT focused core subjects and Computer Science.
  • Secondary (Class 9 & 10): Specialized focus on core subjects with IT integration.
  • Senior Secondary (Class 11 & 12): Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, and Humanities streams.

Enrollment Process

Admissions for the upcoming academic session are strictly merit and availability based. Parents are encouraged to visit the school reception or submit an online query below.

Mandatory Documents

  • Original Birth Certificate (for Pre-Primary)
  • Valid Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous institution
  • Aadhaar Card copies of student and parents
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
